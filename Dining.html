Dining

The Church Brew Works a beautiful restaurant with classic stained-glass windows and great pizza. This is not a place you go to just because you like their food, you go for the experience. Surrounded by gothic architecture and large brewing equipment, I could not distinguish whether I was in church or a brewery and it was awesome.











Taste of India Some of the best Indian cuisine can be found right across from the Children's Hospital. It does not seat many, so the intimate dining experience will be like no other.






Coca Cafe an urban cafe with hearty and unique servings. They have whatever you're in the mood for - whether it be sweet or savory, or a fresh-squeezed juice. If your go-to breakfast place is Pamela's, I highly suggest you reconsider. Although you won't escape the wait, you'll enjoy a decadent meal that you never thought you would like!
