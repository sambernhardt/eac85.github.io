Activities

Arsenal Bowl Although I have never been here, this place looks really cool from the outside. And after some research, I think the inside follows suit. They offer affordable deals like $8 all you can bowl nights, 50 cent shoe rentals and bowling alley food. You can enjoy their DJs, live bands, and game nights with a crowd thats 21 and up.





Row House Cinema This is what originally brought me to Lawrenceville. This cool cinema plays a set of specific films each week. They are usually older and there are weeks with themes. With Halloween coming up, they are showing a handful of holiday-related movies to get you into the spirit. We have a cinema like this in my hometown and the one in Lawrenceville is only different because of its hipster vibes and its partner store - Bierfest. It made me nostalgic and I will definitely return.
