about

I am a software engineer based in Pittsburgh, Pa. I enjoy getting lost in the creation of new things and using tech to assist me. Whether I am designing a personal workout program in Java, coding a Twitter bot in python, or upating my website, I am constantly designing and learning. After graduating from the University of Pittsburgh in April 2019 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, I began working for the development team at Mckesson Corp.

experience

I work in Java and SQL on a daily basis. I have developed helpful skills from working in an agile development. As a part of the defect team, I have learned how to debug and implement from more practical code.

Bentley Systems
I enhanced the company's website by developing a code playground for their iModelJS team. The iModelJS team is responsible for managing a javascript library that accompanies their software for infrastructure. It will also be used in tutorials to teach their users more about how to incorporate all their libraries.
